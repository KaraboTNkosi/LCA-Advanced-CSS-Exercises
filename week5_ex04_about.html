The Heart of Eccentric

Eccentric Jewels is a passion project founded in the vibrant heart of South Africa. Jewellery made by South African Creatives for South African Creatives.

Our founder believes that jewelry should not just be an accessory, but a story device, a conversation starter, and a statement. Pieces are designed to be layered because no amount of jewelry is enough jewelry.
